### Observations

1. **More than half of all functions are thin delegates** — the service extraction is already well advanced, but `settlement_game.gd` retains wrapper functions for every service method
2. **88 unique callables form the hard coupling** — these methods must stay (or be replaced by service-to-service calls) during decomposition
3. **~100 proxy variables** just forward to `ui_manager` and `world_setup` — these are the lowest-hanging fruit
4. **`_ready()` is 630 lines** of service configuration — can be split by domain
5. **`_process()` is 60 lines** — already relatively clean, just needs the called methods extracted
6. **Save/load is ~250 lines** at the bottom — self-contained, easy to extract
